Subscription FAQs

Why was I charged by CamScanner?

We understand that the unclear charges may have caused you some trouble. We will do our best to assist you in dealing with this situation. You may be charged for the following reasons:

1. Uncanceled Free Trial

You may have subscribed to the premium free trial service, but did not cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the end of the trial.

2. Active subscription renewal

Your premium subscription automatically renewed because it was not canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

When you subscribe, a window will pop up to remind you that this is an automatic renewal, and it will also display the renewal date, price, plan, and cancellation policy. After purchase, App Store or Google Play Store will also send you an email clearly stating the renewal date and how to cancel the subscription. If you're unsure about your subscription status, please check through the CamScanner app. Open the CamScanner app and go to Me > Account > Subscription Management to verify your current membership status.

Also, please ensure no one else has used your account or device to activate a subscription.

How do I confirm if I have a CamScanner Premium Membership subscription?

Check your active subscriptions in your device's subscription management page:

iOS users

Go to iPhone Settings or App Store > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Android users

Open Google Play, go to Profile icon > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions.

Huawei users

Go to Settings > Account centre > Payment and purchases > Subscriptions

You can also check your membership status in the CamScanner app by going to the Me > Account > Subscription Management.

How do I cancel auto-renewal?

You can follow these steps below to manually turn off auto-renewal and avoid future charges. Simply deleting or uninstalling the app will NOT cancel your subscription.

iOS Users: You can cancel it from App Store or via https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039, and then initiate the refund via Request a refund for apps or content that you bought from Apple - Apple Support. To prevent automatic billing, you shall cancel your subscription at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

If Google play, please cancel it from Google Play or via http://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.

If Huawei, please cancel it from Huawei AppGallery or via https://consumer.huawei.com/uk/support/content/en-gb16002494/

After cancellation, premium features will not expire immediately and will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You can continue using premium features until this period ends.

How can I get further assistance?

Please contact our customer support team through the CamScanner app: For Android users, go to Me > Feedback. For iOS users, go to Me > More settings > Suggestion & Feedback. You can also email us at support@camscanner.com, and a specialist will reach out to assist you.
